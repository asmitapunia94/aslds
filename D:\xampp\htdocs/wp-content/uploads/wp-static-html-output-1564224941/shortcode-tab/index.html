About Us

ASLDS is engaged in skill development training initiative of the Govt.of India.The company provides skill development training to the weaker sections of society in collaboration of various Sector Skill Councils under The Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneureship,Govt of India.We are also implementing training programs funded under CSR by PSUs.
