ABOUT US

ASMITA SKILL LEARNING AND DEVELOPMENT SOLUTIONS (ASL&DS) PVT. LTD is a Delhi-NCR based company working in areas of skill development and capacity building. ASL&DS has conducted skill development awareness programs, mobilized and enrolled m0re than 2000 trainees. ASL&DS has also built strong industry linkages for successful placements of the students through its quality monitoring of bright students via discussion rounds, domain expertise and educational/professional qualification.
