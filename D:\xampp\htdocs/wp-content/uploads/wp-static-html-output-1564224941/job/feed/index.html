Work on future versions of WordPress, Simplenote, and other native apps for iOS and Android. Were looking for an experienced web developer to work on a major new product line. As an early member of this team, you will play a key role in building out the architecture, developing the application and adding innovative data visualizations.You’ll have the opportunity to work as a full stack developer, from the API services and database access layer, up through client side JavaScript.

 
WE ARE LOOKING FOR CANDIDATE WITH

  • Strong programming skills in Objective-C, Java, or both, with deep knowledge of development for iOS and/or Android.
  • Substantial mobile experience developing user interfaces, low-level systems (like storage and networking), or both.
  • Experience developing and launching at least one high quality app.
  • Familiarity with backend technologies and how to interface with them. Backend development experience is a plus.
  • Opinionated about products, with good user experience instincts.

 
HOW TO APPLY
 
Does this sound interesting? If yes, please send a short email to jobs@thisdomain telling us about yourself and attach a résumé. Let us know what you can contribute to the team. Include the title of the position you’re applying for and your name in the subject. Proofread! Make sure you spell and capitalize WordPress and Automattic correctly. We are lucky to receive hundreds of applications for every position, so try to make your application stand out. If you apply for multiple positions or send multiple emails there will be one reply.
